## üìà Understanding the RED Chart (Current Scenario)

### What It Shows
This is your contest performance if you change **nothing**.

### The Shape Explained
```
Day 1-3:   Slow start (üìâ) - People are just discovering your contest
Day 4-7:   Fast growth (üìà) - Word spreads, more people join
Day 8-10:  Slowing down (üìä) - Most interested people already joined
```

This is how **all contests naturally behave**:
- Slow at the beginning (building awareness)
- Fast in the middle (viral spreading)
- Slow at the end (running out of new people)

It's called an "**S-curve**" because it looks like the letter S.
